Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Introduction

mxnifestdreams ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website mxnifestdreams.co (the "Site") and use our coaching services.

1. Information We Collect

Email Subscriptions

When you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Subscription timestamp
  • Device information (browser, IP address — anonymized)

Coaching Sessions

When you book a coaching session through Calendly, the following information is collected:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Session preferences and notes
  • Payment information (handled securely by Calendly/Stripe)

Automatic Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and your use of the Site:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Device information (operating system, device type)
  • Usage data (how you interact with the Site)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our coaching services
  • Sending newsletters and educational content
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Processing payments and confirming bookings
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our Site
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security

3. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data transmitted to our Site is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • Email submissions are encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security protocols
  • Payment information is processed by secure, PCI-compliant providers (Calendly, Stripe)
  • We do not store payment card information on our servers

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services that may collect information:

Formspree

Handles email form submissions. Data is encrypted and GDPR-compliant. See their privacy policy: formspree.io/privacy

Calendly

Manages booking and scheduling. Your information is handled according to their privacy policy: calendly.com/privacy

Substack

Hosts our newsletter and paid community. See their privacy policy: substack.com/privacy

Beacons

Hosts our digital products and free guide. See their privacy policy: beacons.ai/privacy

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our Site uses minimal tracking. We use:

  • Essential cookies for site functionality (HTTPS session tokens)
  • Analytics (optional — used to improve Site performance)
  • We do NOT use invasive tracking or third-party advertising cookies

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails at any time (link in every email)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

7. GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We comply with all GDPR requirements:

  • We only process data with your consent
  • We have legitimate reasons for processing
  • We keep data only as long as necessary
  • We protect your data with appropriate security
  • You can exercise your GDPR rights anytime

8. CCPA Compliance

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to delete personal information collected
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information

We do not sell your personal information.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Email subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe
  • Coaching records: 1-2 years for business/legal purposes, then deleted
  • Payment records: As required by law (typically 7 years)
  • Usage analytics: 90 days by default

10. Children's Privacy

Our Site and services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child, we will delete such information promptly.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: mxnifestdreams@gmail.com
  • Website: mxnifestdreams.co

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Site constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
