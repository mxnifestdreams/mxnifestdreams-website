Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

mxnifestdreams ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information in compliance with GDPR and French data protection laws.

2. Who We Are

Business: mxnifestdreams
Contact: mxnifestdreams@gmail.com
Location: France (EU)

3. What Personal Data We Collect

During Booking (Calendly):

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Booking date and time

During Coaching:

  • Messages and voice notes via WhatsApp, Instagram, or TikTok
  • Topics discussed (manifesting goals, personal situations)
  • Any personal details you share

Email List (Formspree):

  • Email address (only if you subscribe)

Website:

  • IP address (via web hosting)
  • Browser type and device (analytics)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data because:

  • Contractual necessity: To deliver coaching services you've booked
  • Consent: You've opted into our email list
  • Legal obligation: To comply with financial and tax laws
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud

5. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Deliver coaching sessions and services
  • Send reminders and follow-ups via Calendly/WhatsApp
  • Communicate with you about your booking
  • Send weekly essays via Substack (if subscribed)
  • Maintain financial records for invoicing
  • Comply with French tax laws

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information for marketing purposes
  • Use your data for purposes other than service delivery

6. Data Retention

  • Coaching session data: Kept for 2 years after last session for legal/tax purposes
  • Email list data: Kept until you unsubscribe
  • Payment records: Kept for 6 years (French legal requirement)
  • Calendly bookings: Retained per Calendly's policy

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Opt out of certain data processing
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data

To exercise these rights, email: mxnifestdreams@gmail.com

We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • End-to-end encryption (WhatsApp, Instagram)
  • Secure password protection
  • Limited access (only Evan accesses coaching data)
  • No public data sharing

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics to understand visitor behavior. We do not use invasive tracking or marketing cookies.

11. Children's Privacy

mxnifestdreams services are not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover we have, we will delete it immediately.

12. International Data Transfers

All your data is processed and stored within the EU/France. No transfers outside the EU occur without explicit consent.

13. Marketing Communications

Email List:

  • Subscription is opt-in (you must actively choose to subscribe)

  • We send weekly essays only to subscribers

We do NOT:

14. Data Breach Notification

If a data breach occurs affecting your information, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR.
