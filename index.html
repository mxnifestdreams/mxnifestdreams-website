FAQ

How does coaching work?
You book a session on Calendly. In the confirmation, you get my contact details. We work together on WhatsApp, Instagram, or TikTok, whichever you prefer. Text or voice notes, your choice. It's direct, it's focused, and it's about shifting how you see yourself so the external reality follows.
How long does it take to see results?
It depends on you. Some clients see shifts in days. Others take weeks. The timeline isn't about luck or "the universe." It's about how committed you are to becoming the new version. The work is internal. Results are external.
Is this the Law of Attraction?
No. I teach conscious manifesting, also known as the Law of Assumption. The difference matters. Attraction says you need to vibrate at a frequency to get things. Assumption says you already have it, and you live from that conviction. One is about changing energy. One is about changing identity. I teach the second.
Can you help me with my specific person?
Yes. Whether it's an SP, a new job, a new life, or anything else, the method is the same. You become the person who already has it. They respond. Everything aligns. The external is always a mirror of the internal.
What if I'm skeptical?
Good. Skepticism is healthy. But skepticism about how results happen is different from skepticism about whether they happen. My clients have results. If you want them too, that's what matters. Not belief. Action.
What's the refund policy?
Sessions are non-refundable once booked. The work we do is real. The commitment needs to be real. If you're serious about change, book. If you're not sure, wait until you are.
